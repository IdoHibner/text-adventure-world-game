===============================================================================
                       G A M E   I N S T R U C T I O N S
===============================================================================

OBJECTIVE:
   - Work together! You control two players. Your goal is to guide both of them
     to the exit door to proceed to the next level.
   - Score points by collecting cookies, solving riddles or bombing golems.
     WATCH OUT: The more time you take, the less points you get!
                You lose points for wrong riddle answers!
   - PAY ATTENTION: Each player has limited health. If one player loses all
                    their health, you will have to restart the game!
   - Each player can carry one item at a time. You can drop items to pick up
     different ones, or to use them (like bombs).






CONTROLS:
  ----------------------------------------------
  | Action       | Player 1 ($) | Player 2 (&) |
  ----------------------------------------------
  | Move Up      |      W       |      I       |
  | Move Down    |      X       |      M       |
  | Move Left    |      A       |      J       |
  | Move Right   |      D       |      L       |
  | Stay         |      S       |      K       |
  | Drop Item    |      E       |      O       |
  ----------------------------------------------

GENERAL KEYS:
  [ESC] - Pause Menu (Resume, Restart Level, or Exit to Main Menu)
  [C] - Enable/Disable Game Color Mode. Toggle in the main menu.
  [S] - Save current game state.
  [L] - Load previously saved game state. Available from the main menu.



GAME ELEMENTS:
  [ W ] Wall: Blocks your path.
  [ K ] Key: Pick it up to unlock doors.
  [ 9 ] Door: Enter it in order to move to the next level.
              PAY ATTENTION: Both players must enter the door!
  [ ? ] Riddle: Answer correctly to remove it. Wrong answer? Try again.
  [ / ] Switch: Flick them in order to accsess the doors.
                (When ON, they look like '\')
  [ @ ] Bomb: Pick it up and drop it to destroy nearby objects.
              WARNING: It explodes after 10 moves! Stay away from the blast! 
              Make sure not to explode critical objects!
  [ ! ] Torch: Light up dark areas to see hidden objects.
               You need to hold it to keep the area lit!
  [ # ] Spring: Jump to push obstacles away and clear your path,
                Or just to get a temporary speed boost!
  [ * ] Obstacle: Blocks your path.
                  Can be destroyed by bombs or moved with springs,
                  or moved by 2 players pushing it together (if it's size is 2).
                  Obstacles of size 1 can also be pushed by a single player.

EXTRA ELEMENTS:
  [ > ] Portal: Step on it to be teleported to its matching portal.
  [ X ] Golem: Avoid it! It moves around and will crush you if it
               lands on your position.
  [ + ] Cookie: Collect it for bonus points!

TIPS:
  - If you are struggling to solve a riddle, think of a different perspective!
  - Be wary of the number of bombs you have! They are limited!
  - If you get stuck, press ESC->R to restart the level. IT WILL NOT RESET
    YOUR HEALTH!
  - Try bombing the golems, it's hard but will grant you bonus points!
